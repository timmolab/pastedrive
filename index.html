PasteDrive is a simple tool that saves your license plate numbers so you don't have to type them every time you register parking.

Many parking websites and apps require you to enter your plate number manually. This means typing it on a small phone keyboard every morning — and risking typos that could lead to a parking fine.

PasteDrive lets you:
• Save your plate numbers once
• Save your parking website URLs
• Tap a plate to copy it and open the parking site in one step
• Paste and submit — done

All data stays on your device. There is no account, no tracking. PasteDrive is open source and free to use.
PasteDrive is a personal convenience tool. It copies your plate number and opens a URL — it does not register or confirm parking on your behalf. Always verify that your parking is successfully registered on the parking website. PasteDrive is provided as-is with no warranty. The authors are not responsible for parking fines, failed registrations, or any other consequences of using this tool.
